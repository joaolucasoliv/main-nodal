# main-nodal
The main repository for the project “Nodal” as a RA for the Professor Mariana Jimena Alegre. 
----------

Professor Mariana Alegre Escorza
Professor at the HGSD, working towards the improvement of quality of life by advancing citizen-led urbanism to address urban inequality, spatial injustice, and climate challenges.
Bridges research, policy, and practice through participatory processes, civic engagement, and cross-sector collaboration. 
Professor Mariana works with Nodal;

Nodal is a community of collective urbanism focusing on potencializing the new urban generation in the region to improve our cities and territories. 
→ They connect professionals, students, public workers, and activists who look for more equitable and sustainable cities. 

Dr. Alegre’s project explores how collective power and collective care among urban changemakers and communities can be sustained over time through the design of digital tools, platforms, and governance models. It combines applied urban research, user and market analysis, and platform development to build a financially sustainable, technology-enabled ecosystem that supports collaboration, learning, and collective action in Latin American cities. The RA will ideally work from now through August, and tasks will include contributing to the design and development of community tools, supporting user and market research, and assisting in the development of a mission-aligned financial sustainability model.
